# Fix speaker note placement on slides 3 and 4

## Problem
Two speaker notes appear on the wrong slide:

- Line 50: `<!-- speaker_note:` (on Slide 3 "Project description", appears on Slide 4)
- Line 71: `<!-- speaker_note:` (on Slide 4 "Context and motivation", appears on Slide 5)

Both use `<!-- speaker_note:` with a space after `<!--`. All other 27 speaker note blocks in the file use `<!--speaker_note:` (no space) and work correctly.

## Root cause
The space after `<!--` likely causes the Marp parser to not recognize the directive as a speaker note, treating it as a regular HTML comment that gets associated with the next slide.

## Fix
Two edits in `code-config-curriculum.md`:

1. **Line 50**: `<!-- speaker_note:` → `<!--speaker_note:`
2. **Line 71**: `<!-- speaker_note:` → `<!--speaker_note:`

